A man called Otto

This movie is a 2022 American comedy-drama film directed by Marc Forster about a bitter old man reluctantly getting involved in the lives of his neighbors. It stars Tom Hanks in the title role and it is a remake of the 2015 Swedish film A Man Called Ove, which was based on the 2012 novel by Fredrik Backman. This heart touching movie shows a little act of humanity and love is all that you need to enliven someone.
